broccoli and tofu stir fry

INGREDIENTS

14 oz extra-firm tofu
2 small heads broccoli
2 green onions
4 tbsp neutral oil

1 piece ginger
2 cloves garlic
3/4 cup vegetable broth
1/2 cup soy sauce
2 tbsp unseasoned rice vinegar
2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
1/3 cup + 1 tbsp cornstarch

2 cups rice
sesame seeds

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Start the rice.

2. Press tofu. 
3. Preheat oven if applicable, 425F. 
4. Break tofu into ~1in pieces and add to a large bowl. add 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tbsp neutral oil, and toss to coat. Add 1/3 cup cornstarch and toss to coat.

5. Bake tofu at 425F for 25 mins in oven or 400F for 18 minutes in air fryer. Turn over tofu halfway through cook time.

6. Cut broccoli into small pieces. Slice green onions and seperate the green and white pieces.

7. Create sauce; into a bowl add the garlic, ginger, white green onion parts, broth, vinegar, sesame oil, and 1 tbsp cornstarch and whisk to combine.

8. When tofu is done or close to done (<12 minutes), heat 1 tbsp neutral oil in a large pan. Add broccoli and season with salt. Cook on medium-high until broccoli starts to char, 4 to 5 minutes. Transfer broccoli to a bowl.
9. Reduce heat to medium. Whisk the sauce again and pour into pan. Bring to a simmer and cook until slightly thickened, ~4 minutes.
10. Add broccoli and tofu to pan and coat in sauce. Once integrated remove from heat or continue cooking for a few minutes as needed.

11. Serve over rice; top with green green onion pieces and sesame seeds.
